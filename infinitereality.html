WHO IS INFINITEREALITY?

Cassie/Cassandra Erin Szer aka “infinitereality” is an eclectic Australian experimental musician, lyricist, sound engineer, composer, graphic/visual artist, and author. She co-founded Truth Field Projection PTY LTD, offering services in music, security, and technology support. She authored the prototype of "Jailbreak the Universe" in 2022, a series of short stories that directly connect to her music. Her collaborations span various creative mediums.

COLLABORATIONS

infinitereality has collaborated with MICHAEL GARZA since they first argued over the complexity of the vocal bridge of "Exit Music" on her personal livestream. Their main collaboration I REMEMBER was co-created, co-written, and co-produced via email and text messages across the world. It was released on September 11, 2019. Since then, he has been a featured artist on many releases by infinitereality, providing background vocals, as well as being credited as a featured performer.

She has also collaborated with BRIAN MINNICK since early 2022, together creating synchronistic video and audio-related projects. He features on several albums and EPs, in which infinitereality chops and screws his unique audio sounds with her own music. VIDEO/AUDIO COLLABORATION

infinitereality also provides both once-off and ongoing paid commission work, creating individualised audio soundscapes for content creators, applications, games, podcasts, documentaries, short films and television. She has created ongoing personalised audio for the independent artist/creator JOULES OPIA/GODDESS JOULES
