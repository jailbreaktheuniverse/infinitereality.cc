ABOUT infinitereality

Cassie/Cassandra Erin Szer aka “infinitereality”, is an eclectic Australian experimental musician, lyricist, sound engineer, composer, graphic/visual artist, and author. She co-founded Truth Field Projection PTY LTD, offering services in music, security, and technology support. She authored "Jailbreak the Universe" in 2022, the first book of a trilogy directly connected to her music. Her collaborations span various creative mediums.

COLLABORATIONS

infinitereality has collaborated with Michael Garza since they first argued over the complexity of the vocal bridge of "Exit Music" on her personal livestream. Their main collaboration, "I Remember", was co-created, co-written, and co-produced via email and text messages across the world. It was released on September 11, 2019. Since then, he has been a featured artist on many releases by infinitereality, providing background vocals, as well as being credited as a featured performer. They have also collaborated on two covers together, and plan to do more. "I REMEMBER (THE FUTURE)

She has also collaborated with Brian Minnick since early 2022, together creating synchronistic video and audio-related projects. The artwork for the album "Jailbreak The Universe", was inspired by a screenshot from one of his videos. He features on "THE INFINITE CUE", "THE CONVOLUTED SESSIONS" and "THE OPERATOR" EPs. On these albums, infinitereality chops and screws his original tracks and unique audio sounds with her own music. First Collaboration Remote Viewing Camp Hero

infinitereality also provides both once-off and ongoing paid commission work creating individualised audio soundscapes for content creators, applications, games, podcasts, documentaries, short films and television. She has created ongoing personalised audio for the independent artist/creator Goddess Joules Opia. For examples of her work please visit Joules Opia's Youtube Channel Cosmic Sleep - Joules Opia ASMR .
